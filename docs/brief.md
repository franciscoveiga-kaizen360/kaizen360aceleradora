## Executive Summary

**Docs Growth CRM** is an AI-powered CRM platform developed exclusively for dental radiology clinics in Brazil. It is a white-label solution from Logyca Tecnologia, customized and commercialized under the Docs Consultoria e Gestão brand.

The **project goal** is to build a high-conversion sales website (long landing page) that presents the platform to clinic managers and administrators, enabling them to schedule a free demonstration directly through the site. The primary revenue model is monthly/semi-annual/annual subscriptions sold in three tiers.

**Core value proposition:** A single platform that centralizes omnichannel patient service, dentist-referral CRM pipeline, autonomous AI agent, workflow automation, and real-time metrics — built specifically for the dental radiology niche by a team with 21+ years of market experience.

---

## Problem Statement

### Current State and Pain Points

Managers of dental radiology clinics face a critical set of operational, financial, and commercial problems that prevent them from scaling:

**Financial:**
- No knowledge of CAC, average ticket, retention rate, or real profitability
- Financial management based on guesswork — no dashboards or real-time reports
- Unpredictable revenue and no structured recurring income

**Operational:**
- Clinic owners are "empresidiários" (imprisoned by their own business) — overworked, unable to take vacations
- Poorly managed schedules: no-shows, idle time slots, constant delays
- Zero automation: everything manual, generating rework and errors
- Processes undocumented — clinic depends on "key people"

**Commercial:**
- No follow-up process with dentists who refer patients
- No structured process for acquiring new partner dentists
- Data scattered across WhatsApp, notebooks, spreadsheets — no CRM
- No active digital marketing — weak online presence
- Non-standardized, slow patient service

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Generic CRM platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, RD Station) are not built for the dental radiology niche. They require heavy customization, offer no niche-specific workflows, and lack the embedded authority that Docs Consultoria brings through 21+ years of experience with 200+ partner clinics.

### Market Urgency

## Proposed Solution

### The Website

A single-page, long-scroll **conversion-optimized landing page** with anchor navigation following the proven journey:
**Problem → Solution → Proof → Offer → Action**

The site will:
1. Communicate the platform's value in the language of clinic managers (not tech jargon)
2. Present all three pricing tiers with transparent pricing
3. Drive visitors to schedule a free demo (primary CTA) or view pricing (secondary CTA)
4. Leverage Docs Consultoria's authority and social proof to overcome purchase resistance

### Why This Solution Will Succeed

- **Niche authority:** Built by an entity with 21+ years and 200+ clinics in the exact target niche
- **Transparent pricing:** Hybrid model with visible prices + demo booking — reduces friction
- **Proven copy framework:** Headlines, pain points, and objection handlers sourced directly from SPIN Selling research
- **Technology stack:** Simple, fast, mobile-first — matching the browsing habits of the target audience

## MVP Scope

### Core Features (Must Have)

- **Hero Section:** Headline, sub-headline, primary CTA (demo booking), secondary CTA (anchor to pricing), social proof bar, platform mockup
- **Problem Section:** 6 pain point cards with icons and copy based on SPIN Selling research
- **Solution Section:** 7 feature cards (Omnichannel, CRM, AI Agent, Flow Builder, Smart Scheduling, Mass Messaging, Social Media Suite)
- **Authority Section:** Docs Consultoria story — 200+ clinics, 21 years, 100% NPS, team photos
- **How It Works Section:** 3-step simplified journey (Demo → Implementation → Autopilot)
- **Pricing Section:** 3 modules (Essencial, Intermediário, Avançado) with full pricing tables, badges ("Mais Popular", "Recomendado"), and demo CTA per plan
- **Testimonials Section:** Carousel with name, clinic, city, photo, and quote
- **FAQ Section:** 6 objection-handling Q&As
- **Final CTA Section:** Power close copy + demo booking button + WhatsApp link
- **Footer:** Logos, legal links, contact, Instagram, "Powered by Docs Consultoria" badge
- **Floating WhatsApp button:** Persistent across all sections
- **Responsive design:** Mobile-first, all breakpoints
- **SEO base setup:** Meta tags, Open Graph, semantic HTML, target keywords
- **Analytics integrations:** Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics/GTM
- **Demo booking integration:** Calendly, Cal.com or Logyca platform link

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Blog or content section
- Client login / portal area
- Multi-language support (PT-BR only)
- Payment gateway (sales closed by commercial team off-site)
- Custom CMS or admin panel
- Chatbot on the website itself
- A/B testing infrastructure

### MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is considered complete when:
1. All 10 sections are live, responsive, and pixel-perfect across mobile/desktop
2. Demo booking CTA is functional and routes correctly to scheduling tool
3. Facebook Pixel and GA4 are firing correctly on all key events
4. Page scores ≥ 90 on Google PageSpeed Insights (mobile)
5. All copy has been reviewed and approved by Docs Consultoria team

## Technical Considerations

### Platform Requirements

- **Target platforms:** Web (desktop + mobile). No native app.
- **Browser support:** Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (last 2 major versions). iOS Safari and Android Chrome priority.
- **Performance requirements:** LCP < 2.5s on mobile, CLS < 0.1, FID < 100ms (Core Web Vitals pass)
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for primary interactive elements

### Technology Preferences

- **Frontend:** HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JS or lightweight framework (Next.js or Astro acceptable if team prefers). No heavy SPA frameworks for a landing page.
- **Backend:** None required for MVP (static or JAMstack)
- **Database:** None for MVP
- **Hosting/Infrastructure:** Vercel, Netlify, or client's own host — TBD. Must support HTTPS, custom domain.

### Architecture Considerations

- **Repository structure:** Single repo, organized by section components
- **Service architecture:** Static site or server-side rendered (SSR) for SEO — no backend services needed
- **Integration requirements:**
  - Demo booking: Calendly / Cal.com embed or redirect link
  - WhatsApp: `wa.me` deep link with pre-filled message
  - Facebook Pixel: Standard events (PageView, Lead on demo booking)
  - Google Analytics 4 + Google Tag Manager
  - Contact form: Email delivery (Formspree, EmailJS, or similar)
- **Security/Compliance:** LGPD-compliant — Privacy Policy page required, cookie consent banner if analytics cookies used
- **Domain:** `docsgrowth.com.br` (preferred) or `growth.docsconsultoria.com` (subdomain fallback)
